# C2 Unlimited Website Plan

## Navigation Structure (reduced from ~25 items to 6)

1. **Services** — what you sell, organized by buyer
2. **Clients & Results** — proof (logos, case studies, testimonials)
3. **Keynote Speaking** — dedicated track for event bookers
4. **About** — Chris, the team, the philosophy
5. **Resources** — book, podcast, blog, intention tracker (all content lives here)
6. **Contact** — with "Book a Free Consultation" as persistent CTA button

---

## Homepage Content Order

### 1. Hero
One sentence that says exactly what you do and who you do it for.

> "Custom sales, leadership, and service training for home improvement retailers and their teams."

**CTAs:**
- "See Our Work"
- "Book Chris to Speak"

### 2. Client Logo Bar
Home Depot, Lowe's, Louver Shop, Repipe Specialists

*This is the single strongest credibility asset — currently buried halfway down the page.*

### 3. What We Do
Three clean cards:
- Custom Training Programs
- Leadership Coaching
- Keynote Speaking

*Each links to its own deep page.*

### 4. Proof
2-3 case study snapshots with real numbers:
> "Trained 1,200 Home Depot associates across 14 regions"

*If numbers don't exist yet, this is the #1 thing worth building.*

### 5. Chris Section
- Short bio block
- Photo
- Quote
- Link to full About page

*He's the differentiator but not the headline.*

### 6. Testimonials
Pull quotes from named people at named companies.

### 7. Final CTA
> "Let's talk about your team" → consultation form

### 8. Resources Strip (near footer)
Book, podcast, blog — supports the sale; isn't the sale.

## Priority Pages to Build

### Services Pages
Must answer: What does a program with C2 actually look like?

**Include:**
- How long
- How many people
- In-person or remote
- What's the deliverable
- What does it cost (or "starting at")
- What's the process
- Sample agenda

### Keynote Page
**Include:**
- 60-90 second sizzle reel at top (YouTube content exists)
- 3-5 signature talk titles with descriptions
- Audience types (associations, corporate, sales kickoffs)
- Past venues
- Tech rider PDF
- "Check Availability" form (event date, audience size, budget range)

*Event bookers have a checklist — give it to them on one page.*

---

## Questions to Answer Before Drafting

1. **Results & numbers** — anything quantifiable from Home Depot, Lowe's, Louver Shop, Repipe work? Trainees taught, revenue lift, NPS improvement, retention?

2. **Testimonials** — do named quotes from clients exist, or need to collect them?

3. **Chris vs. C2 brand question** — one site or two? Changes everything downstream.

4. **What's actually selling right now** — which offering brings in the most revenue? That should get the most real estate.
